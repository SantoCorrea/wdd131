Site Name

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about the services offered by AutoCare Mechanic, a car repair and maintenance business. The website will serve as a platform for customers to learn about the services, schedule appointments, and contact the business for inquiries.

Scenarios

  1. Customer wants to learn about the services offered by AutoCare Mechanic.
  2. Customer wants to schedule an appointment with AutoCare Mechanic.
  3. Customer wants to contact AutoCare Mechanic for inquiries.
