Pará, Brazil

Pará is a state in northern Brazil, located in the heart of the Amazon region. It's the second-largest state in the country, home to vast rainforest, the Amazon River, and a rich blend of indigenous, African, and Portuguese cultures, with Belém as its vibrant capital. Brazil, the largest country in South America, is known worldwide for its diverse landscapes, lively culture, and role as steward of much of the Amazon rainforest.
